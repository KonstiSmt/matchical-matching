# Process Map: Sales, Staffing, Management

## Confirmed Facts
- Matchical supports an end-to-end operating flow across sales, staffing, and management-adjacent steps.
- Core center of gravity is consultant staffing to client demands.
- Core process path is mostly the same across ICP modes.
- ICP-specific differences are mainly focus points and depth of usage per stage.
- Core path:
  1. Intake consultant data.
  2. Intake client demand data.
  3. Structure, score, and match.
  4. Produce candidate output (including CV export).
- Matchical has adjacent operational modules but is not positioned as full ERP.

## Assumptions
- None currently after this review pass.

## Notes
- Stage-level emphasis differs by ICP:
  - ICP 1: internal consultant operations focus.
  - ICP 2: external demand response speed and quality focus.
  - ICP 3: partner orchestration and final submission control focus.

### Draft Stage Model
#### Sales
- Capture opportunity context.
- Track demand intake.
- Prepare submission-ready candidate package.

#### Staffing
- Identify eligible consultants.
- Evaluate fit and shortlist.
- Coordinate candidate progression.

#### Management
- Maintain operational visibility across engagements and outputs.
- Support reporting and insights tied to staffing outcomes.
