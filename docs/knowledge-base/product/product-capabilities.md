# Product Capabilities

## Confirmed Facts
- Consultant data ingestion:
  - CV parsing from uploaded consultant documents.
  - Internal and external consultant profiles.
- Consultant profile dimensions include:
  - Skills and roles.
  - Certificates.
  - Languages.
  - Education and training.
  - Project and engagement context.
- Opportunity and demand support:
  - Parsing of client requests/demands from uploaded files.
  - Distinction between opportunity context and consultant-specific demand.
- Matching core:
  - Structured role/skill and requirement matching.
  - Ranking outputs for candidate selection workflows.
- CV export:
  - Template-driven export.
  - Multilingual output support.
  - Demand-aware optimization and justification workflows.
- Controlled AI design:
  - Strict workflow-based AI processing.
  - Handcrafted prompts and multi-step processing.
  - User-editable outputs in UI.
